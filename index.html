WELCOME TO STTI SONY SUGEMA

STTI SONY SUGEMA is the best and widest tertiary institution in Karawang.
There are Informatics Engineering majors that are ready to prepare you to be a successful person

Visi

The future of the Sekolah Tinggi Teknologi Informatika Sony Sugema (STTISS) : Making STTIS a center for informatics universities in Indonesia that has a competitive advantage globally.

Misi

➤ Producing graduates who have academic ability and have professionalism in implementing, developing and expanding information technology.
➤ To produce graduates who are able to conduct professional studies/research to solve information technology problems in society.
➤ Producing graduates who can implement their expertise in their fields to meet the needs of society in the global market.
➤ Meet the needs of industry and government for IT workforce.
➤ Providing education and training services in the field of IT.
➤ Develop computer science according to the times.
➤ Have the ability and spirit of entrepreneurship.
